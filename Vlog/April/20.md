# April 20th 2026

We started working on our project today, we will try to post daily updates for the remainder of the time until our first competition, which is the WRO Online Open Championship qualifier for mexican teams. It is currently Monday, April 20th, and we'll have to upload our submission by april 25th I'm pretty sure, so we only have about 5 days to create our robot. At this time, we will focus on completing our drivetrain, troubleshooting our sensors, dedcide which electronic components to use, and hopefully complete the Open Challenge. Depending on the progress we make along the week, we will shift our efforts into tackling the Obstacle Challenge, but our main first objective is completing the Open Challenge.

## What we did today

- Mechanical Systems
  - Initial chasis built out of lego for quick prototyping.
  - Ackerman steering for tight turns.
  - Rear wheel drive assembled with a gearbox reduction. 

- Electronic Systems
  - Troubleshooting with our IMU, motor driver and esp32 to check everything worked correctly.
  - Motor control using H bridge.
  - Direction servo calibrated to our minimun and maximum values for rotation.
  - ESP32 with shield for easier control of our servo and sensors.
  - Verified communication with our ESP32 for quick code iterations.
  - Initialy decided to use ultrasonic sensors, current iteration has them but will be replaced by infrared lidar sensors.
  - Initial PID control for turning at wall detection and trayectory correction.
